Mac N Cheese

Description

Who doesn't love a nice bowl of creamy and steamy Mac N Cheese?

Our recipe is to die for we hope you'll agree!

The ingriedients are listed below, check them out and give us a review.

Ingriedients

  • Elbow noodles
  • Your favorite cheese(s)
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt the cheese down
  2. Add some milk
  3. Season the cheese and milk mixture
  4. Boil the noodles
  5. Combine noodles and sauce
  6. Optional: Add additional cheese
  7. Bake in oven @350 for 30-45 mins until cheese is golden brown
