Fried Chicken

Description

You have NEVER had fried chicken this good before!

You'll be the envy of the potluck with this recipe.

Ingriedients

  • Chicken (wings or breasts)
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the chicken
  2. Crack and beat the egg
  3. Season the egg mixture
  4. Season the flour
  5. Dip chicken in egg then flour
  6. Optional: Repeat for added crunch
  7. Fry in HOT oil until golden brown
